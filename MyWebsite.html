  • Fall 2013

    Every journey has a beginning

    I graduated high school on June 2013 feeling all excited about going to Lehman College not knowing where this would take me. As a first-generation student, I felt lost and not sure about what major I would choose.

  • January 2018

    A Choice Is Made

    After a monotonous first semester studying Macro Economics, I came to the realization that my election was not really fulfilling me or making me feel enthusiastic, I felt disappointed. While doing homework with a friend of mine, I got asked my opinion about his project, and all I saw was a bunch of lines which turned into a simple but entertaining game. This created a spark in me which turned into a flame. I made a choice after bombarding my friend with questions.
    My new major from Macro Economics changed to Computer Science

  • June 2017

    Closing Of A Chapter

    After four years studying at Lehman which were composed of many sleepless nights, stressful days, and challenging classes, I finally graduated on June 2017 with a B.S. in Computer Science wondering what I would be doing next.

  • September 2017

    Here We Go Again

    I was entrusted with a scholarship that would help me continue my education beyond a bachelor's degree. In the last weeks of my stay in lehman, my focus was placed on three different categories: Apply to different master's programs, finish my senior thesis, and finish my last classes as undergrad. After a few weeks, I received a letter from Brooklyn college congratulating me on my acceptance to its computer science master's program.
